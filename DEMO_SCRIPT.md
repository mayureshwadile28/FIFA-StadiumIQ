## 🎯 Act 2: The CommandCenter Demo (0:45 - 2:00)

### 🖥️ On Screen: 
Command Center Tab with the live heatmap updating and streaming alerts appearing.

### 🖱️ Action: 
- Point at the live heatmap where sections are updating.
- Point at the live Match Phase badge at the top (e.g., Gates Open, Halftime, etc.).
- Locate a sector that is **Red (Critical)** or **Black (Over Capacity)** (Halftime is the best phase for this!).
- Click the **"Get AI Recommendation"** button in the sidebar.

### 🗣️ What to Say (Your Speech):
> *"Right now, we are looking at our live Organizer Command Center. This dashboard streams real-time telemetry from all 12 major stadium zones using highly optimized WebSockets. As crowds shift, sections instantly change colors from safe green to critical red and black.*
> 
> *Look at this alert: 'North Lower A' is reaching critical capacity. Let's see how our AI helps organizers make split-second decisions.*
> 
> *(Click the "Get AI Recommendation" button now)*
> 
> *Instantly, our system leverages the power of GenAI to analyze the live stadium state and generate a structured, actionable safety plan. We get a real-time severity classification, the exact bottleneck location, and three specific tactical directives—such as dynamic gate rerouting and steward deployment by name. This transforms passive monitoring into active, life-saving decision support."*

## 💬 Act 3: The Fan Assistant Demo (2:00 - 3:30)

### 🖥️ On Screen: 
Click and switch to the **"Fan Assistant"** tab.

### 🖱️ Action: 
- Select **Spanish (Español)** from the language selector.
- Click the message input field.
- Type: *"¿A qué hora pasa el próximo metro?"* and press Enter.
- Select **English** from the language selector.
- Type: *"Is there a step-free way to enter?"* and press Enter.

### 🗣️ What to Say (Your Speech):
> *"Next, let's look at the spectator experience. StadiumIQ Fan Assistant is a multilingual, context-aware chatbot designed for international World Cup fans.*
> 
> *Let's test it in Spanish. I am going to ask, '¿A qué hora pasa el próximo metro?'*
> 
> *(Type/send the Spanish query and wait 1 second for the reply)*
> 
> *Observe how the AI immediately detects the language, translates on the fly, and replies in natural Spanish. More importantly, it isn't giving generic advice. It reads our real-time transit data and tells the fan exactly how many minutes are left before the next Stadium Central train departs!*
> 
> *Now, let's test our accessibility-first mandate. I'll switch back to English and ask: 'Is there a step-free way to enter?'*
> 
> *(Type/send the accessibility query and wait 1 second for the reply)*
> 
> *Notice how the AI immediately prioritizes our accessible gates, Gate 4 and Gate 8, offering step-free navigation, wheelchair positions, and guiding the fan directly to our support volunteers."*
